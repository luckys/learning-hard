### Typography

- **Headings**: Inter, bold.
- **Body**: Inter, regular.
- **Code**: Fira Code, monospace.

### Spacing

Usaremos la escala basada en 4px de Tailwind:

- `xs`: 4px (p-1)
- `sm`: 8px (p-2)
- `md`: 16px (p-4)
- `lg`: 24px (p-6)
- `xl`: 32px (p-8)
